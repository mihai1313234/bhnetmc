Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Minecraft server

  1. The use of hacks is prohibited and the caught player will be banned.
  2. Using VPN to change IP and profit is strictly prohibited.
  3. Taking advantage of the /tpahere command. To use to teleport a player and kill him will be strictly prohibited and punish the player by banning for a week.
  4. The use of ALT Accounts is prohibited to prevent spoiling the gameplay.
  5. Using bad language towards a player is strictly prohibited and is punishable by 4 days of silence.
  6. Each player is welcome to disable tpa by command /tpatoggle or /tptoggle.
  7. A moderator/staff/helper who does not show up when called will be sanctioned in the discord/minecraft server.

Discord server

  1. It is not allowed to ping continuously without a good reason.
  2. It is not allowed to threaten the user! It is punished with a timeout of 7 days.
  3. If you want to rank-up and help the community, you must have 3 days on the server.
  4. You do not have permission to share your discord server. It is punished by going down to a higher rank and being muted for 2 hours.

More rules

  1. It is not allowed to use Hack-Clients because you infect your computer.
  2. It is not allowed to use bots to slow down the servers and spoil the gameplay of the players.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
